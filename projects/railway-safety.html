Railway Safety Analysis

This project analyzes over 20 years of Canadian rail incident data to uncover patterns, regional risks, and trends in occurrence types. Data was cleaned and pre-processed using Python and SQL, followed by visualization in Tableau to aid in data-driven safety insights.

Key deliverables include exploratory analysis, geospatial risk mapping, and interactive dashboards tailored for rail safety investigators and policy teams.
